Hipster Evolution

Features

This four-function pedometer has the same functions as the Hipster Classic. It measures: * Steps
* Distance in km or miles
* Kcal
* 12/24 clock

In addition to these four functions, however, the Evolution has a G-sensor (top of the line tri-axis technology), meaning the pedometer can be worn around the neck (lanyard included) or put in a pocket or bag. The orientation does not matter as this pedometer accurately measures every step. It also comes with a holder that allows it to be worn on the hip if that method of wearing the pedometer is desired.

This pedometer has a six-step buffer intentionally built into it. This feature means that incidental steps (under 6) are excluded pushing the user on to more purposeful, life saving activity. According to a study of 54,000 men and women by Dr. Steven Blair, the number one predictor of premature death ahead of smoking, high cholesterol, high blood pressure, obesity and diabetes was low cardio-respiratory fitness, which can be mitigated by 30 minutes of brisk walking every day!
